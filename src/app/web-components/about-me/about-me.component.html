About Me

Let me introduce myself

Hi! I'm Juan Reyes, a Mechatronics Engineering graduate from McMaster University with a strong passion for building complex systems that sit at the intersection of hardware, software, and human centered design.

I currently serve as a Technical lead on the McMaster Exoskeleton team, where I guide a team of 6 students through the embedded system architecture and firmware development for a lower-limb robotic exoskeleton. In this role, I've learned how to translate high-level goals into concrete technical direction, mentoring teammates, reviewing designs, and ensuring our software and electronics integrate reliably as a complete system. Previously, I held the role of Control Systems Lead, where I helped derive the exoskeleton's system equations and controller models, including adjustments for human applied forces and real world dynamics.

Beyond academic teams, I spent 16 months at Accipiter Radar, where I developed a strong appreciation for full system ownership and independent learning in a professional engineering environment. There, I worked across disciplines, designing and testing electronics, validating radar hardware, and contributing to PCB design for new systems. I also led and supported software projects, including full-stack web development for customer portals, database design and testing for internal hardware and software BOM automation tools, and performance analysis of PostgreSQL systems. Alongside technical work, I supported quality assurance initiatives, contributing to training development, documentation, and internal audits under an ISO aligned quality management system.

Across all of these experiences, what drives me most is the challenge of learning quickly, adapting to new technologies with minimal guidance, and seeing how individual components come together into reliable, real-world systems. Whether I'm writing embedded firmware, designing control algorithms, or building backend infrastructure, I'm motivated by curiosity, collaboration, and the satisfaction of solving difficult problems end to end.
